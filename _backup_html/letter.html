My Sincerest Apology

Written with love and reflection • October 24th, 2023

Dearest you,

I have spent the last few hours trying to find the right words, sitting here with a cup of tea that has long gone cold. Words often feel too small to bridge the gaps we sometimes create between each other, yet they are all I have right now to reach out to you.

I want to start by saying I am truly sorry. I am sorry for the silence that followed our conversation, for the words I left unsaid, and for the moments where I failed to see things through your eyes. It was never my intention to cause you pain, but I realize now that intent doesn't always mitigate the impact of one's actions.

You deserve someone who listens with their whole heart, who honors your vulnerability, and who stays present even when things get difficult. In that moment, I wasn't that person. I let my own fears and insecurities cloud my judgment, and for that, I am deeply regretful.

"Love is not just a feeling, but a consistent choice to understand, even when understanding seems impossible."

I miss our laughter—the kind that makes our eyes water. I miss the way the world felt a little softer when we were on the same page. More than anything, I miss the trust that we built so carefully, stone by stone, and I am willing to do whatever it takes to help rebuild it, at whatever pace you need.

There is so much more I want to say, but I will start with this: I value you more than I can express. You are a light in my life, and I am committed to being better, not just for us, but because you deserve nothing less.

With all my love and sincerity,

Always yours.
